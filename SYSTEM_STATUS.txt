🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Problem: Red dot (disconnected)
Solution:
  1. Refresh page (F5)
  2. Wait 2-3 seconds
  3. Should turn green

Problem: Login fails
Solution:
  1. Check green dot is showing
  2. Use exact credentials (copy-paste)
  3. Check console (F12) for errors

Problem: Blank page
Solution:
  1. Make sure using: http://localhost:3000/index.html
  2. Not: file:///... or app.html
  3. Refresh with Ctrl+F5
