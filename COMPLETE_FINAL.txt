📋 HOW IT WORKS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1 - Teacher Registers Student:
  • Login as Teacher
  • Click "Add Student" button
  • Fill student details
  • Upload student photo
  • System generates credentials automatically
  • Give credentials to student

STEP 2 - Admin Sets Timetable:
  • Login as Admin
  • Go to "Timetable" tab
  • Click "Add Timetable"
  • Add class schedule (day, time, subject)
  • Students can now see their schedule

STEP 3 - Student Marks Attendance:
  • Login as Student
  • Go to "Attendance" tab
  • Click "Start Camera"
  • Face detection starts
  • System checks:
    ✓ Face match with registered photo
    ✓ Liveness detection
    ✓ Distance within 20m of teacher
  • Attendance marked (pending verification)

STEP 4 - Teacher Verifies:
  • Login as Teacher
  • Go to "Attendance" tab
  • See pending verifications
  • Verify or reject each entry
  • Check for proxy attendance

STEP 5 - Analytics:
  • View dashboard statistics
  • Check at-risk students
  • Generate reports
  • SMS sent to parents
